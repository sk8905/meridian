Meridian Macro — demonstration prototype. Indicators are drawn from official public sources: FRED (US Fed funds rate, core CPI, wages, unemployment, 2-year Treasury), the ONS (UK core CPI, wage growth, unemployment), the Bank of England (UK Bank Rate and 2-year gilt) and DBnomics (US ISM Services PMI); the UK Services PMI figure is from S&P Global/CIPS. Each tile links to its source. The Commentary, Cycle and Bubble views are compiled summaries of public analyst commentary and official data — educational only, not investment advice. Verify against the linked sources before relying on any figure.
