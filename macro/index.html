Meridian Macro — demonstration prototype. Indicators are fetched live from official public sources: FRED (US Fed funds rate, core CPI, wages, unemployment, 2-year Treasury, and the UK Bank Rate), the ONS (UK core CPI, wage growth, unemployment), the Bank of England (UK 2-year gilt) and DBnomics (US ISM Services PMI); the UK Services PMI figure is from S&P Global/CIPS. Each tile links to its source — verify against it before relying on any figure.
